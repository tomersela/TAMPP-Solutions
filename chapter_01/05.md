### Core idea

A parity/XOR-based strategy works.

Let:

- red = $1$
- blue = $0$

The last prisoner in line, who can see everyone in front, announces the XOR of all visible hat values. That answer may or may not match their own hat, so this prisoner may be sacrificed.

After that, each prisoner can combine:

- the parity announced by the first prisoner
- the answers already spoken by prisoners behind them
- the hats they can still see in front

to deduce their own hat color exactly.

### Outcome

Using this strategy:

- at most **one** prisoner may die
- all remaining prisoners can be saved

The key idea is that the first prisoner communicates one bit of global information: the parity of the visible hats.
